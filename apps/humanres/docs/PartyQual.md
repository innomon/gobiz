# Party Qualification Context

A `PartyQual` record represents a qualification held by a party (individual or organization).

## Lifecycle
Qualifications are typically added to a party's profile to track their educational background, certifications, or professional experience.

## Relationships
- **Party**: The individual or organization holding the qualification.
- **Party Qualification Type**: Defines the type of qualification (e.g., Degree, Certification).
- **Status**: Tracks the status of the qualification (e.g., Completed, In Progress).

## Key Fields
- **Party ID**: The party who holds the qualification.
- **Title**: The title of the qualification or degree.
- **From Date**: The date when the qualification was obtained or started.
