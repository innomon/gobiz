# Product Context

`Product` is the central entity for all items sold or used in the system.

## Lifecycle
Products can be of different types and can be categorized into various categories.

## Relationships
- **Product Type**: Defines the nature of the product (e.g., Finished Good, Service, Raw Material).
- **Primary Category**: The main category the product belongs to.

## Key Fields
- **Product ID**: Unique identifier for the product.
- **Product Name**: The display name of the product.
