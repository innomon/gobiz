# Contact List Context

`ContactList` is a collection of contact information used for marketing outreach, such as email newsletters, direct mailings, or telemarketing.

## Use Cases
- **Newsletter Subscription**: Managing parties who have opted in to receive updates.
- **Customer Segmentation**: Grouping customers based on behavior or demographics for targeted outreach.

## Key Fields
- **Contact List ID**: Unique identifier for the list.
- **List Type ID**: Category of the list (e.g., Email, Phone).
- **List Name**: Human-readable name.
- **Description**: Notes about the list's source or intended use.
